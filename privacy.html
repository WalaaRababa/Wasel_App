Privacy Policy

Video Tracker by Wasel
Last Updated: December 2025

Video Tracker ("the App") is developed and operated by Wasel. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data collected through the App. The App is designed solely to access and analyze LinkedIn Page analytics for internal reporting purposes. We do not collect, store, sell, or share any personal information about users.

1. Data We Access

The App may access the following data from LinkedIn:

  • Page insights and analytics such as impressions, views, engagement, and follower metrics
  • Data related only to the LinkedIn Page for which we have administrative permissions
Important: No personal user data is collected or stored.

2. How We Use the Data

The accessed analytics data is used only for:

  • Internal reporting and analysis
  • Performance tracking and monitoring
  • Improving content strategy

We do not use the data for advertising, distribution, or sharing with any third party.

3. Data Storage

We do not store any personal data. Analytics data may be used temporarily for generating internal reports, but it is not stored long-term or shared externally.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, disclose, or share any data with third parties. Your privacy and the confidentiality of LinkedIn analytics data are our top priorities.

5. Security

We take reasonable steps to ensure the protection of any analytics data accessed through the App. No personal or sensitive user data is collected, minimizing security risks.

6. LinkedIn Compliance

Video Tracker complies with LinkedIn's API Terms of Use and Data Privacy policies. We only access data for LinkedIn Pages for which we have valid administrative permissions.

7. Contact Information

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or the App, please contact us:
